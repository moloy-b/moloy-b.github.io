• What's your take on the DBC/EDA experience?
    • EDA is breaking traditional norms and is the future of learning. It's about time other educational institutions take cue and start providing for what is an ever changing job market.
  • What are your impressions?
    • I feel its a great platform where I will get to challenge myself and stretch my limits. The programmes focus on teamwork and importance of perseverance, empathy and self-awareness are areas which I am keen to explore.
  • How do you see yourself engaging with this type of culture?
    • I see myself engaging by contributing and sharing what I learn.
  • Have your expectations of EDA changed? If so, how?
    • My expectations remain the same. I wish to learn, grow and contribute towards common good.
  • Are you excited to participate in this kind of learning environment? Does it make you nervous?
    • I am excited to be a part of the EDA experience. I am happy to take small steps at a time and progress to the next level. It does get challenging at times but I am learning to be more patient.
