• What learnings did you take from the chapter?

  • Emotional intelligence teaches us to be calm. It helps us create conditions for our own sustainable happiness. As French writer and Buddhist monk Matthieu Ricard explains that happiness arises from an exceptionally healthy mind and not a mere pleasurable feeling, a fleeting emotion or a mood. It is an optimal state of being and requires emotional balance alongside a subtle understanding of how our minds function. I learned how we all have a set point of happiness that we return to whenever the euphoria of a pleasant experience or the sting of an unpleasant experience fades out. Many of us assume this set point to be static, however the opposite is true. The set point is movable and can be acheived through proper training.
